Simple Spoonge Cake

Ingrediants

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self raising flour

Notes of Ingrediants

Castor sugar
Finely granulated white sugar
self raising flour
A pre mixed combination of flour and leavening agents(usually salt and backing powder).

Mathod

  1. pre heat oven to 190'c
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium bowl,whip togheter the eggs and castor sugar until fulffy.
  4. Fload in flour.
  5. Pour mixed into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven,or until the top of the cake springs back when lightly pressed.
  7. Cool in the pain over a wire rack.
